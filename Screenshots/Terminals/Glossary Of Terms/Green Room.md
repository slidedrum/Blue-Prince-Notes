#room/green 
![[Pasted image 20251222093533.png]]
Floorplans that have a green border are known as Green Rooms. These rooms are associated with outdoor areas and gardens, and are naturally synergistic with one another.

- A type of outdoor focused rooms
- Often has [[Shovel]], [[Sledgehammer]] or [[Metal Detector]]
- [[The Terrace]], [[The Patio]], [[The Veranda]], [[The Morning Room]], [[The Greenhouse]], and [[The Secret Garden]] are only draftable on the perimeter of the house along the East and West Wings.
- Only the [[The Courtyard]] and [[The Cloister]] can be drafted in the center of the house.
- Depending on the exactly placement of the room, the yield will be different. see [[Gardener's Logbook#^fcfc84|The Soil Survey]]
