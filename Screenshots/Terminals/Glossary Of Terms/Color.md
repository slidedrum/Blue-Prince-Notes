The color of a [[floorplan]] will usually indicate the type of room:  
Violet for [[Bedroom|Bedroom]], 
Orange for [[Hallway|Hallway]], 
Green for [[Green Room|Green Room]], 
Yellow for [[Shop|Shop]], 
Red for [[Red Room|Red Room]]  

and Blue for Blueprints (default type)
