	- [[The Red Prince]]
	- [[Swim Bird]]
	- [[A New Clue]]
- Mother of [[Simon P. Jones]]
- Wears a [[moon pendant]] necklace
- Likes birds
- Writes to mom
- Real name "Mary Matthew Epsen"
- daughter of [[Lady Clara Epsen]] and [[Simon H. Sinclair]]
