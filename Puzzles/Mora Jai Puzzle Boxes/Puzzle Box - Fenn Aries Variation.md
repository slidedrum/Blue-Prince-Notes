	- The [[Fenn Aries]] Variation
	- At the end of [[the war of the Fifth Era]], after the Grand Realm of [[Fenn Aries]] was established, [[Mora Jai]] boxes of the realm were graced with RED TILES in honor of [[General Teskin - King Teskin|General Teskin]]'s victory.  
	- When these tiles are pressed, all BLACK TILES on the box turn RED in triumphant victory, while all WHITE TILES on the box turn BLACK serving as the outclassed and struggling reinforcements of the dwindling [[Desilets]] Army.
