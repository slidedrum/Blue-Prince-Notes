	- Erajan Affix Pairings
	- Prefix
		- Aj - first
		- Ma - Small
		- Ov - Toward
		- Nev - New/The start of
		- El - I/me
	- Suffix
		- Ja - Last
		- Am - Large
		- Vo - Away
		- Ven - Old/The End OF
		- Le - A lack of myself
	- When paired with a verb can mean I will or won't (similar to UL/LU)
- ![[Pasted image 20251218153145.png]]
	- Erajan Vocabulary
	- Early erajan did not use ant verbs, so today the rare usage of verbs are comprised almost entirely of loan words and are mainly used to  describe physical actions.
	- Lor - Word
	- Hew - House
	- Trei - Tree
	- Yait - Gate
	- Lune - Water
	- Dau - Day
	- Nai - Night
	- Arei - Future
